Visit Places - Sheikh Pura Qadeem

VISIT PLACES

Explore the important places of our village along with their photos and descriptions.

Village Tomb

HAZRAT Sayyed Latafat Ali Shah Sabri

He is the one whose tomb is at Huzoorpeer Chowk; the Oliya-e-Ikraam of Saharanpur.

Village Tomb

Dargah Hazrat Sayyed Moh. Alam Nawaz R.H Aaley

The most revered elder saint of the village, whose tomb is near the Idgah.

Old Market

A bustling market where villagers shop for daily needs and local specialties.

Village School

The primary school where children learn and participate in cultural activities.

Playground

Where children and youth play sports and celebrate village events together.

River Side

The river flows gently past the village, providing water and scenic views.

Fertile Fields

Lush green fields where villagers grow wheat, rice, and vegetables.

Old Bridge

An old bridge connecting parts of the village, popular for walks and photography.

Small Temple

A small temple known for its peaceful surroundings and devotional activities.

Community Hall

Used for village meetings, weddings, and social gatherings.

Historical Site

Marks the rich history of the village with old structures and monuments.

Village Pond

A calm pond where villagers relax and children play.

Village Library

Contains books for students and adults promoting education.

Children Play Area

A safe space for children to enjoy outdoor activities and games.

Health Center

Provides basic healthcare facilities and medical aid to villagers.

Village Garden

A beautiful garden with flowers and benches for relaxation and picnics.

River Crossing

A scenic spot where villagers cross the river and enjoy the natural beauty.

Cultural Center

Hosts cultural programs, dance, music, and art workshops.

Main Temple

The central temple of the village known for grand celebrations during festivals.

Secondary School

Provides secondary education and extracurricular activities to the youth.
