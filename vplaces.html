VISIT PLACES

Explore the important places of our village along with their photos and descriptions.

Village Tomb

HAZRAT Sayyed Latafat Ali Shah Sabri

His tomb is at Huzoorpeer Chowk; he is the Oliya-e-Ikraam of Saharanpur.

Village Tomb

Dargah Hazrat Sayyed Moh. Alam Nawaz R.H. Aaley

Most revered elder saint of the village, tomb located near the Idgah.

Old Market

Bustling market where villagers shop for daily needs and specialties.

Village School

The primary school where children learn and participate in cultural activities.

Playground

Where children and youth play sports and celebrate village events together.

River Side

Gently flowing river providing water and scenic views for visitors.

Fertile Fields

Lush fields where villagers grow wheat, rice, and vegetables.

Old Bridge

Connects different parts of the village, popular for walks and photos.

Small Temple

Known for peaceful surroundings and devotional activities.

Community Hall

Used for village meetings, weddings, and social gatherings.

Historical Site

Marks the rich history of the village with old structures.

Village Pond

A calm pond where villagers relax and children play.

Village Library

Books for students and adults promoting education in the village.

Children Play Area

Safe space for children to enjoy outdoor activities.

Health Center

Basic healthcare and medical aid for villagers.

Village Garden

Beautiful garden with flowers and benches for relaxation.

River Crossing

Scenic spot to cross the river and enjoy nature.

Cultural Center

Hosts programs, dance, music, and art workshops.

Main Temple

Central temple known for grand festival celebrations.

Secondary School

Provides secondary education and extracurricular activities.
