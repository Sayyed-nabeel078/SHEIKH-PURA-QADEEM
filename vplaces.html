Old Market

A bustling market where villagers shop for daily needs and local specialties.

Village School

The primary school of the village where children learn and participate in cultural activities.

Playground

A place where children and youth play sports and celebrate village events together.

River Side

The river flows gently past the village, providing water and a scenic view for visitors.

Fertile Fields

Lush green fields where villagers grow wheat, rice, and vegetables for local consumption.

Old Bridge

An old bridge connecting different parts of the village, popular for walks and photography.

Small Temple

A small temple known for its peaceful surroundings and devotional activities.

Community Hall

Used for village meetings, weddings, and social gatherings for all residents.

Historical Site

Marks the rich history of the village with old structures and monuments.

Village Pond

A calm pond where villagers often come to relax and children play.

Village Library

Contains books for students and adults promoting education in the village.

Children Play Area

A safe space for children to enjoy outdoor activities and games.

Health Center

Provides basic healthcare facilities and medical aid to villagers.

Village Garden

A beautiful garden with flowers and benches for relaxation and picnics.

River Crossing

A scenic spot where villagers cross the river and enjoy the natural beauty.

Cultural Center

Hosts cultural programs, dance, music, and art workshops for the village.

Main Temple

The central temple of the village known for grand celebrations during festivals.

Secondary School

Provides secondary education and extracurricular activities to the youth of the village.
