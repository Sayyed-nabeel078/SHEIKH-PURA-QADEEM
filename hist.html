👎👎INTRODUCTION

Sheikh Pura Qadeem is a historic village located in Uttar Pradesh. It has a rich cultural and educational heritage...

Shekhpura Kadeem is a village in Saharanpur district,

Uttar Pradesh, India, known for its annual Urs (fair)

celebrating the Sufi saint Khwaja Shah Alimuddin Chishti.

The area is part of the Saharanpur tehsil and is locate

within the geographical region of the district. The village

also has a post office with the

pincode 247001 and a local elementary school.


👎👎Why was the name changed?

The name of this village was changed

from Fakir Pura to Sheikhpura Qadeem

in the year 1500. When the revered saint

, Dada Peer, arrived here, some villagers

went to meet him. Since then,

an annual Urs (spiritual fair)

is held in his honor, and he

gave the village its new name.
